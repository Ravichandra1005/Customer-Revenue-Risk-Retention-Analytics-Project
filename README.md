# 🚀 Customer Revenue, Risk & Retention Analytics Project

---

## 📌 Overview
Analyzed customer behavior, revenue trends, returns, and delivery performance using Python and Pandas to generate business insights.

---

## 📂 Data Preparation
- Converted Excel sheets → CSV files  
  - customers.csv  
  - orders.csv  
  - order_items.csv  
- Merged datasets using `customer_id` and `order_id`

---

## ⚙️ Feature Engineering
Created new features:
- order_value  
- delivery_delay_days  
- is_repeat_customer  
- is_high_value_order  
- customer_value_segment  
- customer_risk_category  
- order_reliability_score  

---

## 🔍 Key Insights
- High revenue customers also show high return rates  
- Risk cities identified: **Mumbai & Chennai**  
- Delivery delays impact repeat customers  
- Clothing & Electronics categories have higher returns  
- UPI shows higher return-related risk  

---

## 📊 Visualizations
- Monthly revenue trend  
- Customer segmentation  
- Return rate by category  
- City vs category heatmap  
- Delivery delay analysis  

---

## ⚠️ Problems Identified
- High return rates from valuable customers  
- Delivery delays in key cities  
- High-return product categories  

---

## 💡 Recommendations
- Improve product quality & descriptions  
- Optimize delivery in delay-prone cities  
- Reduce returns in Clothing & Electronics  

---

## 🛠 Tools Used
- Python (Pandas, NumPy)  
- Matplotlib, Seaborn  

---

## ⭐ Summary
End-to-end data analytics project covering data cleaning, feature engineering, analysis, and visualization to drive business decisions.
